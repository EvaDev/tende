About this app

How the Merchant app fits into the platform.

A separate app, not a separate service

/merchant is its own React/Vite app (port 5175, alongside consumer 5173 and admin 5174) — but it talks to the same Express backend on port 3001. There is no separate merchant service or database; route files are mounted on the existing server (memberAuth.routes.ts, settlement.routes.ts, merchantSelf.routes.ts).

Why a separate app

A merchant is a business with staff turnover and shared tills — a different trust context from an individual consumer. The merchant app has no wallet, no wagmi, no RainbowKit. Custody (one corporate account) is split from access (many operators with passkey login and roles) and execution authority (settlement above a threshold needs second approval).

Org / member model

A merchant can have many operators (merchant_members), each with a role:

RoleCan do
cashierRing up Point of Sale, view Sales and Products (read-only)
store_managerSame as cashier (reserved for future store-scoped limits)
org_admin (head office)Team, My Business, Products catalog, settlement approval above threshold

Operators sign in with a passkey (WebAuthn) — not a Safe wallet signer. First login is a claim ceremony via invite ID + email.

Custody — why no new wallet was needed

Receiving needs no signature (trustedCounterparty push). withdrawToExternal is onlyRole(ADMIN_EXECUTOR_ROLE) — backend-signed, no merchant private key. Head-office settlement approval is an off-chain rule before that call.

Settlement threshold & approval

Any operator can request a payout. At/below threshold it executes immediately; above threshold a different org_admin must approve. Settlement resolves fiat currency to pegged on-chain token (e.g. ZAR → TTZA).

Origin note for WebAuthn

WEBAUTHN_ORIGIN is a comma-separated list so consumer (5173) and merchant (5175) can complete passkey ceremonies against the same backend.

Change vouchers (digital change at till)

When a customer would receive coins as change, a cashier can issue a change voucher — credit to their wallet. Add a product with delivery type Voucher.

StepWhoWhat happens
1Cashier (POS)Issue change voucher → QR or Send to @tag
2aCustomer (QR)Receive → scan change voucher QR
2bCustomer (link)Open deep link in Receive
2cCustomer (@tag)Direct credit to @tag
3On-chainDebit merchant claim, credit consumer (15 min QR expiry)
